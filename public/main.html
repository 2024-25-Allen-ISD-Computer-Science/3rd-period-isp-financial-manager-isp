About this Site

This website aims to make managing your finances easily understandable and accessible. This project is currently a work in progress and we will aim to continue to add more features as it continues. This site will eventually be able to track basic financial history, show and manage your income and expenses, and provide financial advice and access to information to improve your financial literacy.
